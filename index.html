Fashion is a term used interchangeably to describe the creation of clothing, footwear, accessories, cosmetics,
and jewellery of different cultural aesthetics and their mix and match into outfits
that depict distinctive ways of dressing (styles and trends) as signifiers of social status,
self-expression, and group belonging. As a multifaceted term, fashion describes an industry, styles, aesthetics, and trends
